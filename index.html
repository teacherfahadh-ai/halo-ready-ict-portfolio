Field Methodology: The Resilience Protocol

My work is guided by the "Zero-Point Infrastructure" philosophy:


  1. Redundancy by Design: Every critical system must have a low-tech or offline backup.
  2. Energy Independence: Configuring systems for low power consumption to survive intermittent grid issues.
  3. Human-Centric Tech: If a user cannot operate it with 5 minutes of training, the system is too complex. I prioritize UI/UX even in backend systems.
